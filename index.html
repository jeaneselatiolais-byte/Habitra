AI that runs entirely on your device

Core ML-powered intelligence. Zero data leaves your phone.

Pattern Detection

Discovers hidden trends in your habit data like day-of-week strengths and completion velocity.

Risk Prediction

Warns you when a streak is at risk of breaking before it happens, so you can intervene.

Mood Correlation

Tracks mood alongside habits and finds which routines drive how you feel.

Weekly Recaps

AI-generated summaries with insights, coaching tips, and motivational feedback.

Health Scoring

Each habit gets a health grade based on consistency, momentum, streak resilience, and engagement.

Habit Correlations

Coaching Messages

Contextual encouragement based on your current performance and upcoming challenges.

HealthKit Insights

Correlates steps, sleep, and heart rate with habit completion to find what fuels your best days.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data stays on your device

Habitra processes all AI and analytics on-device using Core ML. We never collect, transmit, or store your habit data, mood entries, or health information on any server. Period.
