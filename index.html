A calmer way to build habits

Build your cairn,
one stone at a time

Cairn is a calm, private habit tracker. Place a stone for each habit you finish today and watch your cairn rise — no streaks to defend, no scores to chase. On your iPhone and Apple Watch. No ads, no accounts, no cloud.

Download on the App Store

Everything you need, and nothing you don't

Quiet by design. One stone at a time.

🪨

Today's Cairn

Place a stone for each habit scheduled today. Tap to place, tap to lift. "3 of 4 placed" — glanceable progress with no streak to defend and no day marked as a failure.

🧭

Look Ahead

The Ahead view shows your rhythm and what's coming up, so you can set a gentle nudge for tomorrow instead of dreading a broken streak.

🔔

Gentle Reminders

Set a quiet reminder for each habit, at the time it actually helps. A nudge, never a nag — and no guilt if you miss one.

⌚

On Your Wrist

A grade-free Apple Watch app and complication. Place a stone right from your wrist and your cairn stays in step with your iPhone.

❤️

Apple Health Auto-Complete

Link a habit to Apple Health — Steps, Workouts, Mindful Minutes, or Sleep — and Cairn places the stone for you when your Health data crosses your goal. Read-only; data never leaves your device.

📱

Home & Lock Screen Widgets

See today's cairn at a glance from your Home and Lock Screen. Widgets update on their own as you place stones.

🔒 Privacy First

Your cairn stays on your device

Cairn keeps everything on your iPhone. No servers, no analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Your habits, your mindful minutes, your health data — none of it ever leaves your device. Period.
