Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Habitra processes everything locally using on-device AI — no servers, no cloud analytics, no third-party tracking. Your habits are your business.

Data We Collect: Habitra does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All habit data, mood entries, streak history, and AI-generated insights are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SwiftData) and, if you opt in, synced via your personal iCloud account.

HealthKit Integration: If you choose to enable HealthKit, Habitra reads health metrics (steps, sleep, and heart rate variability) solely to provide habit correlation insights. This data is never transmitted off your device or shared with any third party. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in your device Settings.

iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud sync, your habit data is synced across your personal Apple devices through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is encrypted in transit and at rest. Jeanese Latiolais does not have access to your iCloud data.

Analytics & Advertising: Habitra contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no tracking pixels. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

On-Device AI: All AI features — including pattern detection, predictive alerts, mood analysis, and weekly recaps — run entirely on your device using Apple's Core ML framework. No data is sent to any external AI service or API.
