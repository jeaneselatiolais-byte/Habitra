4. HealthKit Data Handling

With your explicit permission, Habitra may read the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count (daily totals)
  • Sleep analysis (duration)
  • Heart rate (resting)
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Workout duration

This data is used exclusively for on-device correlation analysis to show how health metrics relate to your habit completion patterns. In strict compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • HealthKit data is read-only — Habitra does not write any data to HealthKit
  • HealthKit data is processed and stored only on your device
  • HealthKit data is never transmitted to external servers, third parties, or advertising networks
  • HealthKit data is never used for marketing, advertising, or data mining purposes
  • HealthKit data is never sold or monetized in any way

You may revoke HealthKit access at any time via iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Habitra.

The Pro Annual plan includes a 14-day free trial. During the trial period, you have full access to all Pro features at no charge. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid annual subscription at $39.99/year. You may cancel the trial at any time before it expires to avoid being charged.

5.4 Auto-Renewal

Pro Monthly and Pro Annual subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your Apple ID account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period at the rate of your selected plan.

You may cancel your subscription at any time. To cancel, go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Habitra and tap Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you will retain access to Pro features until that date. No partial refunds are provided for unused portions of a billing cycle.

The Pro Lifetime plan is a one-time purchase of $79.99 that grants permanent access to all Pro features. This is not a subscription and does not renew. The Lifetime purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed into the same Apple ID.
