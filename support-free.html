Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a habit?

Tap the + button on the Today tab. Enter a name, choose an icon and color, set your frequency (daily, weekdays, weekends, or custom days), and tap Save. Your new habit will appear in your daily list immediately.

How do I complete a habit?

On the Today tab, tap the circle next to any habit to mark it complete. You'll feel a satisfying haptic tap and see your streak update. To undo, tap the circle again before midnight.

How do I edit or delete a habit?

Long-press any habit on the Today tab to open the context menu. From there you can choose Edit to modify the habit, Archive to hide it without losing data, or Delete to permanently remove it and all its history.

Can I track a habit multiple times per day?

Yes! When creating or editing a habit, set Times per Day to your target (e.g., 8 for water, 2 for skincare, 3 for medication). Each tap adds one completion. A progress ring fills as you go, showing your count (e.g., "3 of 8"). Your streak counts the day as complete only when you hit the daily target. Tap the completed habit to reset all completions for the day.

How do streaks work?

Your current streak counts consecutive scheduled days where you completed the habit. If you miss a scheduled day, the streak resets to zero. Your longest streak is always preserved. Milestones are celebrated at 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 90, 100, 180, and 365 days.

Will Habitra have AI features?

Yes! On-device AI coaching is on our roadmap. When it ships, all AI processing will happen privately on your phone. Follow us for updates.

Will Habitra have mood tracking?

Mood tracking with journal entries and sentiment analysis is planned for a future update.

Does Habitra connect to Apple Health?

Yes. When creating or editing a habit, open the Apple Health section and link it to a source (Steps, Workouts, Mindful Minutes, or Sleep). Habitra auto-completes that habit once today's Health data crosses your threshold. Access is read-only — Habitra never writes to Apple Health and no health data ever leaves your device.

How do I set up the widget?

Long-press your iPhone home screen, tap the + in the top corner, search for "Habitra", and add the widget. It shows your daily habits and completion progress at a glance.

Will Habitra support iCloud sync?

iCloud sync is coming soon! We're working on seamless cross-device syncing via CloudKit. For now, your data is stored securely on your device.

Is Habitra really free?

Yes! Habitra is 100% free with no ads, no paywalls, and no data collection. You can optionally leave a tip ($1.99, $4.99, or $9.99) to support development, but tips don't unlock anything.

I left a tip. Can I get it back?

Tips are one-time consumable purchases processed by Apple. For refund requests, contact Apple Support through the App Store.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Go to Settings and toggle Dark Mode. Habitra supports both light and dark themes with colors optimized for readability in each mode.

What are Badges and how do I earn them?

Badges are earned by hitting milestones like streak counts (7, 14, 30, 100+ days), perfect days, perfect weeks, and seasonal achievements. View your badge collection in the Stats tab under Badge Shelf. There are 50+ unique badges to collect.

How does the XP and leveling system work?

You earn 10 XP for each habit completion, with bonuses for streaks and perfect days. As you level up, you unlock recognition for your consistency. XP popups show your progress after each completion.

Can I share my streak achievements?

Yes! From the Stats tab, tap any streak to open the share card. Choose from multiple gradient themes and share directly to Messages, social media, or save the image.

Troubleshooting

Notifications aren't arriving

Open your iPhone's Settings > Notifications > Habitra and ensure notifications are enabled with Banners, Sounds, and Lock Screen turned on. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them.

Widget shows stale data

Widgets update on iOS's schedule. Try removing and re-adding the widget. Ensure both the Habitra app and HabitraWidget share the same App Group in Settings > Signing & Capabilities.

App crashes on launch

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store. As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app.
