Description
Habitra is the habit tracker that actually learns how you work. Powered by on-device AI, it spots your patterns, predicts when you're about to slip, and nudges you at exactly the right moment — all without ever sending your data to a server. YOUR HABITS, YOUR DEVICE, YOUR PRIVACY Every insight Habitra generates stays on your phone. No cloud AI, no API calls, no tracking. Your habit data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it across your own Apple devices with iCloud. SMART AI COACHING • Predictive alerts: "You usually skip Fridays — want a reminder?" • Habit correlation insights: "Your workouts improve when you sleep 7+ hours" • Mood check-ins with sentiment analysis • Weekly AI recaps generated locally on your device BEAUTIFUL WIDGETS FOR EVERY SURFACE • Lock Screen — see today's streak at a glance • Home Screen — habit grid and progress bars • Standby Mode — full-screen display while charging • Live Activities — active habit timers on your Lock Screen BUILT FOR YOUR HEALTH Connect to Apple Health to unlock powerful correlations between your habits and your wellbeing. Steps, sleep, and heart rate variability data fuel smarter insights — all processed on-device. FLEXIBLE & FREE TO START Track up to 3 habits with streaks, stats, reminders, and a Lock Screen widget — completely free, forever.
