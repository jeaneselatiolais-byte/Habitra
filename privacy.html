Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

🔒

Habitra is built with a privacy-first architecture. All data processing happens on your device. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your personal data.

1. Overview

Habitra ("the App") is a habit tracking application for iOS developed by Jeanese Latiolais ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Habitra is designed so that your habit data, mood entries, health information, and personal patterns never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to sync via iCloud.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Habitra does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following on external servers:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Habit names, completions, or streak data
  • Mood entries or sentiment analysis results
  • Health data from Apple HealthKit
  • AI-generated insights, predictions, or coaching messages
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

4. Apple HealthKit

If you grant permission, Habitra reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count — daily step totals
  • Sleep analysis — sleep duration
  • Heart rate — resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV) — stress and recovery indicators
  • Workout duration — exercise session length

This data is used exclusively for on-device correlation analysis to show how health metrics relate to your habit completion patterns. In compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • Health data is never sent to external servers
  • Health data is never used for advertising or marketing
  • Health data is never shared with third parties
  • Health data is never sold or monetized in any way
  • Habitra does not write any data to HealthKit

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time via iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Habitra.

5. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync in the App's settings, your habit data, completions, mood entries, and categories are synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is:

  • Stored in your private iCloud account, not on our servers
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Accessible only by you on devices signed into your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

iCloud Sync is entirely optional and disabled by default. You can disable it at any time in the App's settings.

6. On-Device AI Processing

Habitra uses Apple's Core ML framework to provide intelligent features including:

  • Pattern detection and habit insights
  • Streak risk prediction
  • Mood-habit correlation analysis
  • Adaptive reminder optimization
  • Health scoring and coaching messages

All AI and machine learning processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is sent to cloud-based AI services, and no AI model training data leaves your device.

8. Notifications

With your permission, Habitra sends local notifications to remind you about habits. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled locally on your device
  • Not routed through any external push notification service we operate
  • Configurable per-habit in the App's settings

You can manage notification permissions in iPhone Settings > Notifications > Habitra.

9. Third-Party Services

Habitra does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics frameworks (no Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Crash reporting services that transmit personal data

10. Data Export & Deletion

Export

You can export all your habit data, completions, and mood entries at any time from Settings > Export Data. Exports are available in CSV and JSON formats and are generated locally on your device.

Deletion

To delete all your data, you can:

  • Delete individual habits (long-press > Delete) which removes the habit and all its completion history
  • Uninstall the App, which removes all locally stored data
  • If iCloud Sync was enabled, also go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > Habitra to remove cloud data

11. Children's Privacy

Habitra does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation, does not collect personal information, and does not include advertising. If you believe a child is using the App and have concerns, please contact us.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we do not collect email addresses or contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA & Global Privacy)

Since Habitra does not collect or store your data on external servers, most traditional data rights are inherently satisfied — your data is already entirely under your control on your device.

For EU/EEA residents (GDPR)

Habitra does not process personal data on external servers, so GDPR obligations regarding data controllers and processors do not apply in the traditional sense. You have full control over your data at all times. You can access, export, or delete all data directly from your device.

For California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Habitra does not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties. We do not collect personal information as defined under the CCPA. There are no categories of personal information to disclose because no data leaves your device.

For all users

Regardless of your location, you can:

  • Access all your data directly within the app
  • Export your data via Settings > Export Data (CSV format)
  • Delete any or all data by removing habits or uninstalling the app
  • Revoke HealthKit or notification permissions at any time via iOS Settings

If you have questions about exercising any privacy rights, please contact us.
