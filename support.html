Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a habit?

Tap the + button on the Today tab. Enter a name, choose an icon and color, set your frequency (daily, weekdays, weekends, or custom days), optionally assign a category, and tap Save. Your new habit will appear in your daily list immediately.

How do I complete a habit?

On the Today tab, tap the circle next to any habit to mark it complete. You'll feel a satisfying haptic tap and see your streak update. To undo, tap the circle again before midnight.

How do I edit or delete a habit?

Long-press any habit on the Today tab to open the context menu. From there you can choose Edit to modify the habit, Archive to hide it without losing data, or Delete to permanently remove it and all its history.

Can I track a habit multiple times per day?

Yes! When creating or editing a habit, set Times per Day to your target (e.g., 8 for water, 2 for skincare, 3 for medication). Each tap adds one completion. A progress ring fills as you go, showing your count (e.g., "3 of 8"). Your streak counts the day as complete only when you hit the daily target. Tap the completed habit to reset all completions for the day.

What are habit categories?

Categories let you group related habits (e.g., Health, Productivity, Mindfulness). You can create custom categories with their own names, icons, and colors in the habit creation form. Filter habits by category on the Today tab.

How do streaks work?

Your current streak counts consecutive scheduled days where you completed the habit. If you miss a scheduled day, the streak resets to zero. Your longest streak is always preserved. Milestones are celebrated at 7, 14, 21, 30, 60, 90, 100, 180, and 365 days.

How does AI prediction work?

Habitra analyzes your completion patterns using on-device Core ML to predict which habits are at risk of being missed. The Predictions view shows risk levels (low, medium, high) and suggests actions. All processing happens locally on your device — no data is sent anywhere.

What is Mood Tracking?

Habitra lets you log your mood daily with five levels (Great, Good, Okay, Low, Rough) and optional notes. Over time, the AI finds correlations between your mood and habit completion, helping you understand which routines improve how you feel.

How does HealthKit integration work?

With your permission, Habitra reads steps, sleep, and heart rate data from Apple Health to correlate your physical health with habit performance. This is entirely optional and all analysis happens on-device. Go to Settings > HealthKit Integration to enable it.

What do the Weekly Recaps include?

Every week, Habitra generates an AI-powered recap with your completion rate, streak highlights, mood trends, pattern insights, and personalized coaching tips. Find them in the Stats tab.

How do I set up the widget?

Long-press your iPhone home screen, tap the + in the top corner, search for "Habitra", and add the widget. It shows your daily habits and completion progress at a glance.

Is my data backed up?

With iCloud Sync enabled (Settings > iCloud Sync), your habits, completions, and mood entries sync across all your devices via CloudKit. You can also export your data as CSV or JSON from Settings > Export Data.

What is Habitra Pro?

Habitra is free for up to 5 habits. Habitra Pro unlocks unlimited habits, all streak themes, advanced AI insights, data export, HealthKit integration, and iCloud sync. Choose monthly, annual, or lifetime plans.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > Subscription and tap Restore Purchases. Your Pro subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Go to Settings and toggle Dark Mode. Habitra supports both light and dark themes with colors optimized for readability in each mode.

What are Badges and how do I earn them?

Badges are earned by hitting milestones like streak counts (7, 14, 30, 100+ days), perfect days, perfect weeks, and seasonal achievements. View your badge collection in the Stats tab under Badge Shelf. There are 50+ unique badges to collect.

What are Quests?

Quests are weekly and monthly challenges that reward you with bonus XP and special badges. Check the Stats tab for active quests. New quests generate automatically so there's always something to work toward.

How does the XP and leveling system work?

You earn 10 XP for each habit completion, with bonuses for streaks and perfect days. As you level up, you unlock recognition for your consistency. XP popups show your progress after each completion.

Can I share my streak achievements?

Yes! From the Stats tab, tap any streak to open the share card. Choose from multiple gradient themes and share directly to Messages, social media, or save the image.

Troubleshooting

Notifications aren't arriving

Open your iPhone's Settings > Notifications > Habitra and ensure notifications are enabled with Banners, Sounds, and Lock Screen turned on. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them.

Widget shows stale data

Widgets update on iOS's schedule. Try removing and re-adding the widget. Ensure both the Habitra app and HabitraWidget share the same App Group in Settings > Signing & Capabilities.

HealthKit data not appearing

Go to iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Habitra and make sure Steps, Sleep Analysis, and Heart Rate are enabled. Then return to Habitra Settings > HealthKit Integration and toggle it on.

iCloud Sync not working

Ensure you're signed into iCloud on your device (iPhone Settings > Apple ID > iCloud). Verify that Habitra has iCloud permissions enabled. Try toggling the sync off and on in Habitra Settings.

App crashes on launch

Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, ensure you're running the latest version from the App Store. As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app (your data will be preserved if iCloud Sync is enabled).
